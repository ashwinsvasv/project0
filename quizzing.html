The Story

I started quizzing at the age of 9 when I was called by my teachers for selections for the inter-school competitions conducted between our group of schools in the city. I ended up getting selected and going for the cluster quiz and answering a picture question with Sania Mirza with just one block showing her earring, thus bagging the third position for our school.

I also participated in the inter-house quiz in 6th standard when my brother defeated me as he was in 12th standard from another house. Once I changed schools in 7th standard, I joined a quiz club in an attempt to improve my quizzing pursuits. I could not do anything except for answer questions which the coordinator asked. In 10th standard my passion for quizzing was revived when one of my seniors selected me for the inter-house quiz which was about Indian Mythology and we defeated all by a huge margin. In 11th standard, I participated in quite a few quizzes and gained fame for being a quizzer in my school as I won the Murugappa Madras Quiz which was famous in the city. In 12th standard, I was selected to be the quiz club head of my school and was in-charge of selecting students to represent the school in quizzes.
