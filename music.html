A song I performed at my school's cultural event



History

I started learning carnatic music under the tutelage of Smt. Chitra Bilvam at the age of 5 in the city of Bangalore. I learnt till the first varnam under her.

After that, I shifted to Chennai and continued to learn music under Smt. Lalitha Subramanian. As the senior batch there were far ahead of me, I was put in a junior batch. I relearnt my geethams and swarajathis and climbed up the ladder to varnam once more. Unfortunately, I had to shift to another place by this time and had to quit these classes. Despite having a break between my Bangalore and Chennai classes, I managed to maintain the flow.

However, when I shifted next, my voice started breaking and my range became lesser. I restarted learning under Shri N. Ganapathyraman from Varnam level. My interest in music increased drastically during this time. I started going to concerts and learning practically.

This phase came to a halt with my 10th standards. I had to prepare for my board exams and with pressure from school teachers, I couldn't continue with music classes. I restarted classes in 11th standard for a short while and now am regulary attending classes online.
